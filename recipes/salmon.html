Alex's Oven Baked Salmon!

Description:

This is a dish that I make all of the time. It's definitely a crowd pleaser. Give it a try and let me know what you think!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Garlic Salt
  • Chipotle Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Sate seasoning
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Pour some soy sauce on the fish
  3. Apply the rest of the seasonings. Just take a guess as to how much. It's really just up to your own tastes.
  4. Place on baking tray and throw it in the oven. Bake until 145 degrees F internal temp. Usually around 15 minutes or so.
  5. Serve with Rice and Veggies
